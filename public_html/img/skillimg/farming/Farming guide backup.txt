<div class="title1">1.0 Introduction</div></a>

<p>Farming has not yet fully been released, so very little is known about what use the skill will be. This guide will continue to be updated as more information about the twentieth skill is released. Jagex have planned to release the farming skill in July.</p>


<p>At the present time, the basis for the farming skill can be found in Draynor Village, with two seed stalls, and two master farmers.</p>

<hr /><Br><br>

<a name="2">
<div class="title1">2.0 The Seeds</div></a>

<p>The Draynor market area now contains two seed stalls, a seed merchant named Olivia, and two master farmers. The seed stalls require  theiving level 27 to thieve from, and the master farmers require theiving level 38 to thieve from. Olivia runs the seed stores, and will buy or sell seeds. Certain tree seeds can also be obtained from bird nests, which randomly fall out of trees while you're woodcutting.</p>

<p>For more information regarding thieving the seeds, see the <a href="http://www.osrs_help.com/skills.php?id=5">Thieving Guide</a>.</p>

<a name="4">
<div class=title1>4.0 Strategies</div></a>
<p>To start off your farming career, you will need to collect seeds, and some farming equipment. There are a few methods to obtain the items required to start farming. These include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Pickpocketing master farmers in Draynor Village markets;</li>
<li>Stealing seeds from the Draynor Village seed stall;</li>
<li>Buying seeds from Olivia, in Draynor; or</li>
<li>Killing Farmers (level-15/17) for seeds and equipment</li>
</ul>
<p>Farmers are scattered throughout RuneScape; making their homes in farms everywhere. Farmers can be found in the below locations:</p>
<ul>
<li>Draynor & Lumbridge: Near the Draynor windmill, and north of Lumbridge chicken coop [ <a href="http://www.osrs_help.com/img/skillimg/farming/lumb.png">Map</a> ]</li>
<li>Falador: On the way to Port Sarim [ <a href="http://www.osrs_help.com/img/skillimg/farming/falador.png">Map</a> ]</li>
<li>Ardougne: Just northeast of the north bank [ <a href=""http://www.osrs_help.com/img/skillimg/farming/ardougne.png>Map</a> ]</li>
</ul>

<p>More will appear when the skill is released.</p>[/html]
